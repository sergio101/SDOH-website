Audio and show notes

We played Gina Brooklyn. If my memory serves me correctly, her parents played in the band Krank, whom we covered for The Village Buzz back in the 90s.

The Village Buzz was a paper-based music magazine that we published in the 90s, before the days of the internet.

I did an Interview with Chris Donley recently. You can see the whole thing Here.

“Daily Life” by Tim Easton and the Feelan Barons played the intro music to “The Midwest Report,” a segment I hosted on KSCB in Santa Barbara, California. When I get a few minutes, I’ll start posting episodes of that show.

I did a music video for The Tony Lang Band. You can see it on YouTube.

The Mike Farley Band will perform a 20th Anniversary show for Halfaworldaway on September 6, 2025. More details will follow.
